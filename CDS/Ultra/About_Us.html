About Ultra

Nature Company Purifying and Bottling Water, Juice and Soft Drinks Ltd. (ULTRA WATER) is a Jordanian company that was established in Amman in May, 2004. Nature Company focused on innovation and business excellence through applying the latest technology in water purification to bring you the healthiest unique high quality water in the world.

Nature Company is the only water in Jordan and the Middle East that is completely free of sodium and impurities. Furthermore, Nature Company is on the verge of launching a new product, Ultra Energy, which is considered to be of the highest quality in the field by using the Vitalizing effect (Revitalizing Water).

At Nature Company, we are proud of our products; and we aim to become even prouder in the future. Our ambitions are limitless; and today, Ultra water brand is associated with quality, efficiency and superior customer service.

We are aiming to become the number one water brand in Jordan and the world, through hard work, dedication and innovation. We have made strategic partnerships to enhance our services and make our products available to our customers not only in the Middle East region, but also all over the world. We will keep enhancing our company, and try to come up with new and innovative products to better suit our customers wherever they are.

Nature Company has obtained the certificate of ISO 22000 and HACCP certification, in addition to the quality certificate of Jordan and FDA – NSF America certification.
